Our Philosophy

That means our boys go to school every morning just like any other kid in the neighborhood. They play sports. They have chores. They eat together. They grow. We don't isolate healing behind walls — we let it happen in the middle of real life.

Why Care Option Exists

Built for the Boys No One Else Was Serving.

From Medicare-Certified home health expertise to Houston's most caring residential program.

Care Option Residential Treatment Center was founded by a healthcare leader with over 15 years of experience running a Medicare-certified home health agency — serving some of the most medically complex patients in the Houston area.

That experience revealed a critical gap: boys as young as 8 falling through the cracks of the system. Too young for most programs. Placement options running out at the exact moment they needed stability the most.

Care Option was built to be the answer to that gap — a home where a boy who feels safe, who goes to school every morning, who has someone in his corner, can actually heal. Not just survive. Thrive.

◆ Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, home-like environment where boys ages 8–15 receive the professional care, educational support, and normalcy activities they need to stabilize, grow, and successfully return to their families and communities.

◆ Our Vision

A Houston where no child waits too long for help — and every boy who needs a safe place finds one.

Daily Life at Care Option

What a Day at Care Option Looks Like

Structure creates safety. Every day is intentional — and every moment is an opportunity for growth, connection, and just being a kid.

Morning · 7:00 AM

Wake Up, Breakfast & School Prep

Boys start with a consistent morning routine — hygiene, a home-cooked breakfast together, and preparation for the school day. Structure here sets the tone for everything that follows.

School Day · 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Local Schools — Just Like Any Other Kid

We provide transportation to and from neighborhood schools. IEP coordination, academic support, and communication with school counselors are handled as a matter of standard care.

Afternoon · 3:30 – 6:00 PM

Snacks, Sports & Organized Activities

After school means snacks, outdoor play, organized sports, arts and crafts, and life skills activities. Every afternoon has purpose — and plenty of room to just play.

Evening · 6:00 – 9:00 PM

Family-Style Dinner, Games & Wind Down

Evenings center on a shared dinner where conversation and connection happen naturally. Followed by board games, reading time, and a consistent bedtime routine that signals safety and predictability.

Weekends

Community Outings, Recreation & Family Visits

Weekends bring community outings, recreation programs, and carefully facilitated family visits. Boys stay connected to the world outside — because that's where they'll eventually return.

Getting Started

How Placement Works

We've made the process as straightforward as possible. Our team is here to guide you every step of the way — with clarity, not paperwork overload.

1

Initial Contact

Call us at (281) 809-3653 or email careoptionrtc@gmail.com. You'll speak directly with our team — not a call center. Ask anything. We have time for you.

2

Intake Assessment

We review the child's clinical history, medical records, school records, and current situation to ensure we're the right fit — and that we can provide what he needs.

3

Placement Decision

We coordinate with the case worker, referring agency, or legal representative to finalize paperwork, authorizations, and transition details efficiently.

4

Move-In & Transition

His space is prepared before he arrives. The first day is calm, unhurried, and designed to reduce anxiety. Families and case workers receive immediate contact once he's settled.

Placement calls accepted Monday–Saturday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
