Why Professionals Choose Us

What We Offer Referring Professionals

When you place a boy with Care Option, you're placing him with a licensed, healthcare-grade team that will communicate proactively and document everything.

Licensed Texas Facility

Fully licensed in the state of Texas for residential youth care. We comply with all applicable regulations and standards for child-placing agencies and residential programs.

Medication Administration

Licensed to administer medications, with meticulous documentation for every prescription. A key differentiator — most Houston homes cannot or do not offer this level of pharmaceutical management.

School Continuity & IEP Support

Daily transportation to neighborhood public schools, IEP coordination, homework support, and direct communication with school counselors and special education staff.

24/7 Trained Staff Supervision

Screened, background-cleared caregivers trained in trauma-informed care and de-escalation. Boys are supervised around the clock by consistent, professional staff — not rotating contractors.

Detailed, Court-Ready Documentation

We generate monthly progress reports, incident documentation, school attendance logs, and medication administration records — all formatted to support court proceedings, case reviews, and placement decisions.

Proactive Communication

Case workers and advocates receive weekly updates — not reactive silence. We initiate communication. You will never have to chase us. Our philosophy: if you have to ask, we already failed.

Step-by-Step

How to Make a Referral

Four steps from your first contact to a confirmed placement. We keep the process fast and professional.

1

Call or Email

Call the referral line at (281) 809-3653 or email careoptionrtc@gmail.com. Briefly describe the child's situation and urgent needs.

2

Share Documentation

Send clinical history, medical records, behavioral assessments, school records, and any legal documentation to our email. Secure transfer available upon request.

3

Receive Our Assessment

Within 24–48 hours we will confirm whether we are a fit, ask any clarifying questions, and share our availability. No delays, no runaround.

4

Finalize & Place

We coordinate directly with your agency to complete authorization, placement paperwork, and transition logistics. You stay informed throughout.

Transparency & Accountability

What We Report — and When

You'll never wonder what's happening with your case. We report proactively, accurately, and in the formats you need.

Monthly Progress Reports

Behavioral, academic, social, and medication summaries — formatted for case file submissions and court reviews.

Incident Documentation

Any incident is documented and reported within 24 hours, following Texas regulatory requirements. No exceptions, no delays.

School Attendance & Academic Records

Weekly attendance logs, grade reports, and communications from school personnel, delivered directly to the placing agent.

Medication Administration Logs

Complete pharmaceutical records available upon request at any time. Suitable for court submission and medical review.

Family Visit Logs

Documentation of every facilitated family visit — date, participants, duration, and general observations.
